# LegalDocs AI 
### Intelligent Legal Document Platform for Indian Contracts

> Upload an Indian legal document and get a clear breakdown of potentially risky clauses, comparison with standard Indian legal references, highlighted concerns, questions worth asking before signing and an overall contract risk score — explained in plain English.

⚠️ Project Status: Currently under development — new features are being added continuously.

---

## Overview

LegalDocs AI is an AI, ML, and NLP-powered legal document intelligence platform designed specifically for Indian contracts.

Whether it’s a loan agreement, rental contract, employment offer, NDA, insurance document or service agreement, the goal is simple: help people understand what they are signing.

Users upload a PDF and the system processes both digital and scanned documents, extracts important information, identifies clauses, highlights sections that may need attention and connects them with relevant Indian legal references.

Instead of reading pages of legal text manually, users receive a structured and easier-to-understand summary of the document.

---

## Why This Exists

Legal documents are often long, technical and difficult to interpret without professional help.

As a result, many people end up signing agreements without fully understanding:

- hidden penalties  
- restrictive conditions  
- financial commitments  
- unusual legal language  
- clauses that deserve clarification  

Existing AI tools are mostly built for general-purpose tasks and are not tailored to Indian legal workflows. They also usually do not provide a complete experience on a single click.

LegalDocs AI brings document processing, AI, Machine Learning, NLP, legal comparison and user guidance together into a single workflow.

# Features

## Current Features 

- Upload legal PDFs
- Support for both:
  - Digital PDFs
  - Scanned PDFs
- OCR support for scanned documents
- Text cleaning and normalization
- Contract type recognition
- Clause extraction
- Named Entity Recognition (NER)
- Indian financial format support:
  - Rs.
  - ₹
  - LPA

---

## Planned Features 

### Clause Risk Highlighting

Each clause will be reviewed and assigned:

- 🟢 Low Risk  
- 🟡 Medium Risk  
- 🔴 High Risk  

along with confidence indicators.

---

### Comparison with Indian Legal References

Contract clauses will be checked against relevant Indian legal frameworks including:

- Indian Contract Act  
- RBI Guidelines  
- Labour Laws  
- Consumer Protection Rules  
- Model Tenancy Act  

to surface conditions that may require attention.

---

### Suggested Questions Before Signing

For flagged clauses, the platform will generate practical questions users may ask the concerned authority.

Examples:

- Can this penalty amount be negotiated?
- Is this notice period standard?
- What happens if I miss a payment?
- Is this clause mandatory?

---

### Overall Contract Risk Score

Every document will receive:

- Risk score (0–100)
- Clause-wise contribution
- Key areas of concern
- Priority review sections

---

### Plain-English Explanations

Complex legal language will be converted into simpler explanations so users can quickly understand:

- what the clause means  
- why it matters  
- what should be reviewed further  

---

### Exportable Report

Generate a report containing:

- Contract type
- Highlighted clauses
- Risk score
- Legal references
- Suggested questions
